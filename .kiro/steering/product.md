# Product Overview

**Healify** (formerly FinPro) is a digital banking and financial services platform that provides comprehensive money management solutions through a mobile-first approach.

## Core Features
- Digital wallet management
- Investment and trading capabilities
- Easy money transfers
- Financial planning and budgeting tools
- Educational resources for financial literacy
- Secure banking with low fees

## Target Audience
Users seeking convenient, secure, and comprehensive digital banking solutions with emphasis on mobile accessibility and financial education.

## Key Value Propositions
- Convenience: Manage finances anytime, anywhere
- Security: Robust security measures with user-friendly interface
- Education: Financial literacy resources and personalized insights
- Integration: All-in-one financial hub for various banking needs
